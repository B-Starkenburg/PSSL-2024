109th Peripatetic Seminar on Sheaves and Logic

November 15th-17th, 2024

Leiden, The Netherlands

About the Event

The 109th Peripatetic Seminar on Sheaves and Logic (PSSL 109) will take place over the weekend from November 15th-17th 2024 at the Kamerlingh Onnes Building of Leiden University. This is intended to be an informal meeting that covers all areas of category theory and its applications. Talks by PhD students and young researchers are particularly encouraged. An overview of previous editions of the PSSL can be found here.

Invited talks

There will be two invited speakers (talk details coming soon):

  1. Paolo Perrone (Oxford University)
  2. Maaike Zwart (IT University of Copenhagen)

Venue

We will gather in the Kamerlingh Onnes Building (room A0.51) of Leiden University.

Registration

There are no registration fees. Please fill out the registration form.

If you wish to give a presentation, please indicate this by sending a brief abstract (at most 1 page, A4 size) to Chase Ford at

m.c.ford [at] liacs.leidenuniv [dot] nl

The deadline for registration and talk submission is 01 November 2024 (AoE).
