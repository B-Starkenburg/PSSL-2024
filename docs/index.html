109th Peripatetic Seminar on Sheaves and Logic

Leiden University, The Netherlands

Date: November 15th-17th, 2024

About the Event

The 109th Peripatetic Seminar on Sheaves and Logic (PSSL 109) will take place over the weekend from November 15th-17th 2024 at the Kamerlingh Onnes Building of Leiden University. Following the tradition of the PSSL, this is intended to be an informal meeting, covering all areas of category theory and its applications. Talks by PhD students and young researchers are particularly encouraged. For a history of the PSSL, please see here.
